My entire multi-decade career has been in software development, including web app development since 2003. Having worked for companies like NCS, Pearson Education, Edline and Blackboard Engage, the K-12 education industry has been my mainstay.

Over the years, I have used the following programming languages: Java, Ruby, Javascript, Delphi and RPG. I've even dabbled recently in Scala.

Most recently I have been a full-stack web developer with a focus on Java. However, I have been very interested in Ruby on Rails and AngularJS and have been developing those skills.

For additional information please visit my Linked In page.

My immediate family includes my wife and three sons, two of whom are gainfully employed in software/web development. My wife spends a great deal of time with our youngest son, who has Kabuki Syndrome.

I enjoy learning about technologies related to software development by tinkering on my own and participating in local meetups.

I prefer to be active, when possible, by riding my mountain bike and participating in various sports. I play tennis year round and am captain of our local athletic club's USTA League tennis team.
